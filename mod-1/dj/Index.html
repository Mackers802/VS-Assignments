Here's what you need to know

  • When the mouse hovers over the square it will turn blue.
  • When the mouse button is held down over the square it will turn red.
  • When the mouse button is let go over the square it will turn Yellow.
  • When the mouse is double clicked in the square it will turn Green.
  • When the mouse scroll is used somewhere in the window it will turn Orange (not just over the square).
  • You can also change the box color by (typing "r" for "red", "b" for "blue", etc...)
